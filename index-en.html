Who we are

A mission at the heart of the Caribbean

MARËIS is a Guadeloupean association committed to protecting the Caribbean marine ecosystem. We work to preserve sea turtles, coral reefs, mangroves, and all the biodiversity that enriches our Caribbean waters.

Our mission: to raise awareness, protect, and take action so that future generations can still admire the wonders of our seabed.
