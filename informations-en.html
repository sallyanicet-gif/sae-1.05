Frequently asked questions

Need help?

How do I become a volunteer?

It's simple! Fill out the contact form by selecting "Become a volunteer" or come directly to our office during opening hours. No special skills are required, just motivation!

Do I need to know how to swim to participate in trips?

For snorkeling trips, it is recommended to know how to swim. However, we also offer observations from the shore or by boat, accessible to everyone. Feel free to contact us to find the right activity.

Can children participate?

Yes! Children are welcome from age 6 for clean-ups (accompanied by an adult) and age 8 for observation trips. We also offer workshops specially designed for young people.

How can I make a donation?

You can donate by bank transfer, check or directly at our office. Donations qualify for a 66% tax reduction. Contact us for bank details or a tax receipt.

Do you offer corporate workshops?

Yes, we offer eco-friendly team-building activities (team beach clean-ups) and awareness conferences for businesses. Contact us for a custom quote.

Our team is available to answer all your questions.
